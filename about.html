Our History

NTG's Bakery was founded in 2021 when Ndamulelo decided to share his legendary recipes with the community. What started as his home kitchen norm quickly grew into a neighborhood favorite.In 2021, we opened our first store in Braamfontein, where we continue to serve customers today. Through the years, we've maintained our commitment to traditional baking methods while incorporating new flavors and techniques.Today, we're still operating, with Ndamulelo and his employees now leading the business into its next chapter while preserving the traditions that made us beloved in the first place.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to elevate the everyday through the art of baking, creating unforgettable flavors from scratch with integrity and passion. To create exceptional baked goods that bring people together, using time-honored recipes, the finest natural ingredients, and a passion for the art of baking.To nourish our local community with hand-crafted artisan bread made from locally-milled grains, creating a warm gathering place and fostering a more sustainable food system.

Our Vision

To be the heart of our community a place where memories are made, traditions are celebrated, and every bite tells a story of quality and care. To prove that the future of food is local and sustainable, inspiring a network of bakeries to rebuild regional grain economies and eliminate food waste. To transform our neighborhood into a destination for food lovers, known for our iconic bakery and the sense of belonging we foster.
