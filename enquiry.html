Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes, we have a variety of gluten-free and vegan baked goods available daily. Please place orders for specific items 24 hours in advance to ensure availability.

How far in advance should I place custom orders?

For custom cakes and large orders, we recommend placing your order at least 72 hours in advance. For weddings and very large orders, please contact us at least 2 weeks ahead of time.

Do you offer catering services for events?

Absolutely! We provide catering for events of all sizes. Our catering menu includes pastry platters, dessert tables, and custom cakes. Contact us for a customized quote.

Can I modify an order after it's been placed?

Order modifications can be made up to 48 hours before the pickup/delivery time, subject to product availability. Additional charges may apply for changes requiring extra ingredients.
